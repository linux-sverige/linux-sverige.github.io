Common Questions

What if I don't receive the confirmation email?

Check your spam or junk folder! Sometimes email providers filter automated messages. If you still don't see it after 10 minutes, contact us at info@linux-sverige.se

Can I cancel during the trial?

Yes! There's no commitment. If you decide EUnion isn't for you, simply let your trial expire. No payment information is required during the trial.

What happens to my data after the trial?

If you upgrade to a paid plan, all your data is preserved. If you don't upgrade, your account is deactivated after 30 days and data is deleted in accordance with our privacy policy.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No! The 30-day trial is completely free. We only ask for payment information when you decide to upgrade to a paid plan.
