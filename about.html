Our Mission

"To provide European businesses and individuals with a cloud platform that respects their privacy, protects their data, and keeps their information within European borders. We believe in digital sovereignty - the right of every European to control their own data."

🎯 Founded on Privacy Principles

Why EUnion Exists

The Problem

For decades, European data has flowed to American tech giants. We use their email, store our files on their servers, and trust them with our most sensitive communications. But there's a cost beyond the subscription fee:

  • The CLOUD Act allows US authorities to access European data
  • Data is used to train AI and profile users
  • European privacy laws are treated as compliance checkboxes
  • Digital dependency on non-European companies

Our Solution

EUnion was built from the ground up as a European alternative. Not a clone of American services, but a fundamentally different approach:

  • All data stored exclusively in EU data centers
  • GDPR-native design, not compliance retrofit
  • Zero-knowledge architecture where possible
  • European company, European law, European values

Our GDPR Commitment

GDPR isn't just compliance for us - it's our core philosophy

📋 Data Minimization

We collect only what's necessary to provide our service. We don't profile you, we don't track you across the web, and we don't sell data to advertisers.

🎯 Purpose Limitation

Your data is used only for the purpose you provided it. We don't repurpose your documents for AI training or use your communications for advertising.

⏱️ Storage Limitation

We keep your data only as long as you need it. When you delete something, it's truly deleted - not just hidden from view.

✅ Lawfulness, Fairness, Transparency

We process data lawfully, fairly, and transparently. You always know what's happening with your data and why.

🔐 Integrity and Confidentiality

We process data securely with encryption, access controls, and regular security audits. Your data's safety is our top priority.

📍 Data Location

All data is stored in certified EU data centers. We never transfer your data outside the European Economic Area.
